001 — The problem

A comment that waits
six hours for a reply
isn't a customer anymore.

Most teams treat social inboxes like email — check it tomorrow, reply when it's quiet. But Instagram and TikTok aren't email. Intent decays in minutes.

FlowBot sits on your accounts and answers the moment a comment or DM hits a keyword you set. Your team wakes up to a contact list, not a backlog.

002 — How it works

Three steps. One afternoon.

STEP 01

Connect your accounts.

One-click OAuth for Meta (Facebook + Instagram) and TikTok. We request only the scopes we need to read comments and send messages — nothing else.

tiktok_creatorconnected
scopespages_messaging, ig_dm
STEP 02

Set your trigger.

Pick the post and the keyword. Or apply a rule across every post on a channel. Match exact words, prefixes, or fall back to AI intent if a comment is messy or off-script.

004 — What it does

The four pieces
that turn a comment
into a conversation.

01·TRIGGERS

A keyword. That's the rule.

Pick the words that mean intent — order, price, link, sign me up, info. FlowBot matches them on every comment and DM, scoped to a single post or your whole account.

  • exact, prefix, contains, regex
  • per-post or account-wide rules
  • AI intent fallback for messy text
