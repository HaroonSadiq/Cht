How to request deletion

You have three ways to remove your data from FlowBot:

  1. Remove FlowBot from Facebook. Open your Facebook settings, go to Business Integrations, find FlowBot, and click Remove. Facebook notifies us automatically and your data is deleted within 30 days. Open Facebook settings.
  2. Disconnect from your FlowBot dashboard. Sign in to your dashboard and click Disconnect on the integration you want to remove. Your data is deleted immediately. Open the dashboard.
  3. Email us. Send a deletion request to mharoonsadiq8@gmail.com. We respond and complete the deletion within 30 days.

What we delete

When we receive a deletion request linked to your Facebook account, we permanently remove:

  • Every Facebook Page you connected through FlowBot
  • Every comment-to-DM rule you set up
  • Every contact and conversation record collected on those Pages
  • The full inbound and outbound message history routed through FlowBot
  • All access tokens and webhook configuration tied to your account

Deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed. We keep only the confirmation record (your code and the date) for up to 90 days, so you or Facebook can verify the deletion happened.
